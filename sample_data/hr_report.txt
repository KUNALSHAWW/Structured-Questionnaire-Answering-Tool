NovaTech Solutions – HR & People Report 2025

EMPLOYEE HEADCOUNT
As of December 31, 2025, NovaTech Solutions had 342 full-time employees and 28 part-time contractors. The engineering department is the largest team with 148 members, followed by Sales & Marketing (72), Customer Success (54), Operations (38), and Corporate Services (30).

EMPLOYEE TURNOVER
The voluntary turnover rate for fiscal year 2025 was 11.3%, down from 14.8% in 2024. The industry average for mid-size tech firms is approximately 13.5%. NovaTech attributes the improvement to the launch of the "GrowPath" career development programme and an across-the-board 4% salary adjustment effective April 2025.

REMOTE WORK POLICY
NovaTech Solutions operates a hybrid model. Employees may work remotely up to three (3) days per week, with core in-office days on Tuesday and Thursday. Fully remote arrangements are available for roles designated as "location-independent" and require VP-level approval. Flexible start times between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM local time are permitted for all non-customer-facing roles.

BENEFITS HIGHLIGHTS
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision) for employees and dependents.
- 401(k) retirement plan with 4% company match.
- 20 days paid time off (PTO) plus 10 company holidays.
- Annual learning stipend of $2,500 per employee.
- Parental leave: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 8 weeks for secondary caregiver.

DIVERSITY & INCLUSION
NovaTech Solutions' workforce is 46% female, 54% male. 32% of leadership positions (Director and above) are held by women. The company partners with Code2040 and Out in Tech to improve representation of underrepresented groups in technology roles.
