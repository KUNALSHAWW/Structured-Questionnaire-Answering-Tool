NovaTech Solutions – Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity Plan
Document ID: DR-BC-2025-003 | Last Updated: January 15, 2026

1. OVERVIEW
NovaTech Solutions maintains a comprehensive Disaster Recovery (DR) and Business Continuity Plan (BCP) to ensure minimal disruption to operations and client services. The plan is reviewed and tested semi-annually.

2. RECOVERY OBJECTIVES
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO): Critical systems must be restored within 4 hours.
- Recovery Point Objective (RPO): Maximum acceptable data loss is 1 hour of transactional data.
- For non-critical systems, RTO is 24 hours and RPO is 12 hours.

3. INFRASTRUCTURE REDUNDANCY
- Primary data centre: AWS US-East-1 (Virginia).
- Secondary (failover) data centre: AWS US-West-2 (Oregon).
- Database replication: Synchronous replication between primary and secondary sites using Amazon Aurora Global Database.
- Object storage: Amazon S3 with cross-region replication enabled.

4. BACKUP PROCEDURES
- Full database backups are taken daily at 02:00 UTC.
- Incremental backups every 30 minutes.
- Backups are encrypted with AES-256 and stored in a geographically separate region (AWS EU-West-1).
- Backup integrity tests are performed weekly.

5. INCIDENT RESPONSE TEAM
The DR/BC Incident Response Team consists of:
- Incident Commander: VP of Engineering
- Communications Lead: Director of Corporate Communications
- Technical Lead: Senior Site Reliability Engineer
- Business Lead: COO

6. COMMUNICATION PLAN
- Internal: Slack channel #incident-response + automated PagerDuty alerts.
- External: Status page (status.novatech.io) updated within 15 minutes of incident declaration.
- Client notification: Email notification within 1 hour for Severity 1 incidents.

7. TESTING & DRILLS
- Tabletop exercises: Conducted quarterly with leadership team.
- Full failover drill: Conducted semi-annually; last drill completed November 2025 with successful failover in 2 hours 47 minutes.
- Results are documented and improvement actions tracked in the DR improvement register.
