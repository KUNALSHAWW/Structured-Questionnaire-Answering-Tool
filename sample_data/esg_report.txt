NovaTech Solutions – ESG & Sustainability Report 2025

ENVIRONMENTAL COMMITMENT
NovaTech Solutions is committed to reducing its environmental footprint. In 2025 the company achieved the following milestones:

Carbon Neutrality: NovaTech achieved carbon neutrality for Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions in September 2025 through a combination of energy efficiency improvements, renewable energy procurement, and verified carbon offsets.

Renewable Energy: 100% of electricity consumed at the Austin and Denver offices is sourced from wind and solar power purchase agreements (PPAs). The Toronto and London offices purchase Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs) equivalent to their consumption.

E-Waste Programme: NovaTech partnered with GreenCycle Inc. to responsibly recycle all end-of-life electronics. In 2025, 4.2 metric tonnes of e-waste were diverted from landfill.

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY
- NovaTech Foundation donated $520,000 to STEM education programmes in underserved communities.
- 87% of employees participated in the annual volunteer day (NovaCares Day).
- Supplier diversity programme: 18% of procurement spend was directed to certified minority-owned and women-owned businesses.

GOVERNANCE
- The Board of Directors includes an ESG Committee chaired by independent director Lisa Park.
- NovaTech publishes an annual ESG report aligned with the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) standards.
- Executive compensation is partially linked to ESG performance metrics (10% weight).
- Whistleblower hotline operated by independent third party; 3 reports received in 2025, all resolved.
